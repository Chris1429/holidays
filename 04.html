Portmahomack

After the lengthy run on busy, narrow roads, a gentle run down the less popular east coast towards Portmahomack was a pleasant change. Starting with a trip to John O'Groats we followed the coastal road, seeing a sea eagle not far before stopping at Brora for lunch.

Yes that name rings a bell, so after lunch we headed for a tour of the Clynelish distillery and their newly refurbished visitors centre before wandering down to Portmahomack for the evening.

We were looking forwards to visiting Portmahomack, thinking it might be an idyllic find, but to be honest it was a bit of a forgotten backwater, and the local inn was not welcoming. Oh well.
