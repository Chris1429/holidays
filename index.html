Our holidays

Over the past few years we have taken several trips around the UK and Europe, on two wheels and four. This is a website dedicated to our trips, please check out the pages for each area.

NC500++

Last year we took a lovely holiday trip to the Scottish Highlands and beyond. We took in the sights of the Great Glen, Ullapool, Dunnet Head, Portmahomack, Banff and finally the Cairngorms. I had wanted to tour the coast of the north of Scotland for a long time, but it had to be on two wheels. It's taken me a while and now it's a "thing" but it was worth the wait!

Le Mans

In many ways the spiritual home of motorsport, a trip to France to cheer on Zarco, Rossi and the other two wheeled gladiators! Attempting to sing La Marseillaise, exchanging greetings with the Gillet Jaunes and getting lost in the madcap campsite.
