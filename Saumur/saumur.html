Nestled alongside the idyllic lower reaches of the Loire River, Saumur provides a fairytale castle, fabulous local shops and market, plentiful local vineyards, world famous display horses and a fantastic armoured vehicle museum.

As well as being a great area to relax, sample some fantastic local food and wine and enjoy some pleasant spring sunshine, Saumur also happens to be about an hours drive from Le Mans...
